# [Invoicelet](https://invoicelet.com) — Free Online Invoice Generator

**[invoicelet.com](https://invoicelet.com)** is a free, open-source invoice generator that runs entirely in your browser. Create professional invoices and export them as PDFs — no sign-up, no server, no fees.

## Features

- **Instant PDF Export** — Generate and download polished PDF invoices with one click
- **18+ Currencies** — USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, INR, GEL, and many more
- **16 Languages** — English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Chinese, Arabic, Georgian, and more
- **Tax Calculation** — Configurable tax rate with automatic totals
- **Version History** — Save, name, search, and restore invoice versions locally
- **Auto-Save** — Edits persist across browser sessions automatically
- **100% Private** — All data stays in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server
- **No Account Required** — Start invoicing immediately

## How It Works

1. Open **[invoicelet.com](https://invoicelet.com)**
2. Fill in your company and client details
3. Add line items with quantities and prices
4. Choose your currency and language
5. Click **PDF** to download your invoice

## Tech Stack

- Pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript — zero dependencies, zero build step
- [html2pdf.js](https://github.com/eKoopmans/html2pdf.js) for client-side PDF generation
- Hosted on GitHub Pages

## Privacy

Invoicelet processes all invoice data client-side — your invoice content never leaves your device. We use Google Analytics to understand general usage patterns and improve the product.
